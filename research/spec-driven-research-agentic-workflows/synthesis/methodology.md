# Methodology

- **Source Triangulation (M06):** Gathered at least two T1/T2 sources for all MUST-level normative statements across Spec-D, E, and F.
- **Contradiction Log (M07):** Maintained active logs of contradictory guidance (e.g., Centralized vs Decentralized docs, ReAct vs DAGs) and downgraded unresolvable MUSTs to SHOULDs.
- **Adversarial Query Expansion (M13):** Executed four axes (Adjacent, Opposing, Abstraction, Orthogonal) for each Spec to escape local minima.
- **Spec-Driven Output Grammar (CB4):** Strictly separated normative statements from rationale, enforcing exactly one RFC-2119 keyword per statement.
